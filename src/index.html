About

Me

Hello, I am Janeth Domínguez, a multidisciplinary artist who loves music, painting, photography and beautiful landscapes. My passion for art was born when I was a child, when I liked to draw, sing and take pictures with my camera. Since then, I have explored different forms of artistic expression, always looking to learn and improve. I like to create works that reflect my personality, my emotions and my vision of the world. My style is varied and original, as I am inspired by different sources and genres. My goal is to share my art with the public and convey a positive and hopeful message.

What kind of art do you do and why?

I dedicate myself to abstract art, especially painting with acrylics and oils. I like abstract art because it allows me to express my freedom and imagination. I am inspired by the world of emotions, dreams and nature. I want to convey with my art sensations, feelings and experiences.

What sets you apart from other artists?

I stand out from other artists by my way of using color, shape and movement. I like to create works that have a balance between chaos and order, between light and shadow, between harmony and contrast. My personality is intuitive, spontaneous and passionate, and that is reflected in my art.

I am an artist passionate about art and creativity. I dedicate myself to creating unique and original works of art, using different techniques and materials. My goal is to express my vision of the world through art and share it with the public.
